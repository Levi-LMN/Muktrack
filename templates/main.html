About Our Family

The Mukuha family is a generational offspring related bloodline, originating from the renowned lineage of Mr. Paul Muiruri Mukuha and Esther Wanjiru Mukuha. Over the years, the family has upheld values of unity and hard work, fostering a strong sense of community. With notable achievements in education and community service, the Mukuha family continues to make a positive impact. As the generations evolve, they strive to honor their heritage while looking towards a future filled with aspirations and opportunities for growth and development.
